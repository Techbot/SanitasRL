Deep below the plains of Ostengot lies the dungeons of Rungaard. Your mission is to retrieve the
long lost crown of King Aaidric. What you might find lies far beyond our imagination. Original, I know.

Your health is permanent in this game, so watch your HP closely. If you lose one point it's lost forever.
You can, however, fortify your health by drinking from a well or by equipping certain items, this
will lower you character's power though.

For each lost health point you grow stronger. It's a game about balancing your character's power and
health pool. There's also various items around the dungeon that will improve your character.

There's a total of five levels in the dungeon with a boss at the end. For more information and downloads,
take a look at the readme.
